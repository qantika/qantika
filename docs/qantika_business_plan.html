Multi-Timeframe Technical Analysis

Revolutionary approach to MACD analysis that combines signals across 1-minute, 5-minute, 15-minute, and 1-hour timeframes with progressive position sizing based on signal confluence strength.

Mathematical Innovation: Progressive Signal Scaling

Instead of traditional binary signals, our system uses:

  • Timeframe State Analysis: Proximity to crossover across multiple time horizons
  • Divergence Mathematics: Quantified strength scoring based on MACD separation dynamics
  • Dynamic Position Sizing: Kelly Criterion approach adapted for signal confluence
  • False Signal Recognition: Machine learning pattern recognition for signal validation

Proprietary Strategies

Volcano Strategy: Time-Based Alpha Generation

Systematic daily LP position creation with wide ranges and pseudo delta-neutral positioning. Positions become safer over time through fee accumulation, creating a self-securing portfolio that generates alpha through compound interest rather than directional betting.

Shooter Strategy: Asymmetric Risk Optimization

Signal-driven LP positions with 10/90 asymmetric ranges and dynamic short protection. Uses revolving capital pool to provide maximum protection during vulnerable early period while positions mature into self-sufficiency.

Data Infrastructure

Real-time blockchain monitoring, historical pattern analysis, and multi-source price feed integration create a comprehensive data foundation that enables sophisticated analysis and rapid decision-making.
