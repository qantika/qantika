What is LP Trading?

Understanding Liquidity Provision Fundamentals

Liquidity Provider (LP) trading is a revolutionary DeFi strategy where you become a market maker on decentralized exchanges. Instead of simply buying and holding tokens, you deposit pairs of assets into liquidity pools, enabling other traders to swap between them. In return, you earn a portion of every trade fee that flows through your position.

In traditional finance, market making was reserved for large institutions with millions in capital. DeFi democratizes this opportunity. When you provide liquidity to a pool like JLP/SOL, you're essentially offering both tokens for traders to exchange, earning fees every time someone swaps. Modern concentrated liquidity protocols like those on DeFiTuna amplify this further – by focusing your capital within specific price ranges, you can earn significantly higher fees than traditional wide-range positions. The key is finding the sweet spot between maximizing fee collection and managing impermanent loss risk, which is exactly what our tools help you optimize.
